GPay Clone App

A Basic GPay Clone for Android

About the App

This is a basic GPay clone app. The app allows users to sign up, log in, transfer money, and view their transaction history.

Account Signup

Create a new account with ease using your email or phone number.

Login

Securely log in to your account using your credentials.

Money Transfer

Send money to other users within the app quickly and securely.

Transaction History

View all your transactions in a clear and organized manner.
